What is Dev Demystified?

There are nearly 1 billion search results for online web development classes. Endless promises and options for you to boost your skills into a new career in two weeks. So why aren’t we all successful web developers?

Dev Demystified has proven that the most effective results are those taught in person, by dev professionals who can equip you with the fundamentals and help you build a mental model that will become the framework for your entire programming career.

Who Is This Curriculum For

Beginner (or not so beginner) developers

Ever read an article or do a tutorial and just can’t get a solution working? Us, too. It’s one of the reasons we designed this curriculum. Maybe you have a little bit of experience writing code. Maybe you’re self-taught. Maybe you’re feeling stuck with where you’re at. This curriculum will help you be a better problem-solver, a more confident team member, and a more successful developer.

Business people who work with developers

Are you working in a role where you depend on a development team but feel like you’re always speaking a different language? Learn the fundamentals of web development in a way that helps you be more effective at your job and better understand the dynamics of a working web development program. You’ll leave with skills that help you better set expectations, advocate for your projects, and deliver better products.

One-Week Intensive Class

  • Learn the fundamentals of web development
  • Get access and experience with all the tools in a professional developer’s toolbox
  • Complete hands on exercises that result in a working web application
  • Get in the moment feedback from experienced developers
  • Leave with the confidence and skills that will result in real world success
